Welcome to SoundBytes!

Click on the buttons on the left side of the screen
to hear what audio samples you can play.

If you like a particular sample, click on a new line
beneath this help guide and write 'add <samplename>'
using the same sample name as on the button. For example,
if you wanted to add the bass(fat) sample you would type
'add bass(fat).'

Either click on the RUN button or use the hotkey CTRL + 'R'
to play the sample or samples that you have added.

If you want to add another sample, click on a new line
and type 'add <samplename>'
